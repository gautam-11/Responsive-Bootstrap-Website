What is cameo?

Cameo is an innovative camera app for both iOS and Android that lets you capture the best moments in life either on still photos or video. Cameo have many useful features to enhance the photos and videos with editing tools, filters and effects. And when you're done you can easily share your result with all of your friends and the rest of the world through Instagram.
